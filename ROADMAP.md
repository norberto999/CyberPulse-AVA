# CyberPulse-AVA Roadmap

## Purpose

CyberPulse-AVA is an internal cybersecurity platform developed to support the security, compliance, and cyber awareness objectives of Avara Foods.

The platform is designed to provide employees, managers, IT Operations, and the Cyber Security Team with a single location for understanding and improving security posture across the organisation.

This roadmap outlines the planned evolution of CyberPulse-AVA to support Avara Foods' strategic cyber security goals.

---

# Guiding Objectives

CyberPulse-AVA will continue to evolve around the following objectives:

- Improve employee cyber awareness
- Increase visibility of security posture
- Support compliance and governance requirements
- Strengthen device security and compliance
- Enhance threat visibility
- Reduce organisational cyber risk
- Improve employee engagement with cyber security initiatives
